ABOUT LOBSTART

$LOBSTAR The viral Solana memecoin that shook the blockchain. An aristocrat in a coat, a crustacean with conviction — and now, a cultural phenomenon immortalized in generative art.

THE TRIBUTE

LOBSTART is a generative art response and appreciation for $LOBSTAR — the Solana memecoin that went viral on pump.fun, capturing the imagination of the entire crypto community with the image of a lobster dressed as a 19th-century aristocrat.

This project celebrates the token's cultural moment by transforming its top 10 holders into algorithmically-generated artworks. Each wallet address becomes a unique specimen — a cryptographic portrait that exists as long as they hold.

When you buy $LOBSTAR, you don't just hold a token. You join a movement. You become part of an ever-evolving living painting on the Solana blockchain.

THE ART ENGINE

Each holder's wallet address is deterministically hashed into a seed that drives the generative art algorithm. The visual output is a cryptographic fingerprint — no two specimens are alike, and the same wallet always generates the same art.

Seven distinct algorithms are assigned by rank, each reflecting a different dimension of the $LOBSTAR mythology: plasma fields like the ocean's depth, sacred geometry for the aristocratic order, orbital systems mapping the token's trajectory through the market.

The art auto-evolves in real-time — every 15 seconds the system polls Solana mainnet. When transactions are detected, specimens flash and regenerate, reflecting the living, breathing nature of the token economy.
