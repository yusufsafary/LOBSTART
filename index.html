ABOUT LOBSTART

THE MANIFESTO

LOBSTART is not just a token — it is a declaration. Born on Solana's pump.fun, dressed in a tailored coat, claws extended toward the horizon of decentralized prosperity.

Every wallet that holds $LOBSTART becomes part of a living ecosystem. The top 10 holders are immortalized as unique crustacean specimens — algorithmically rendered, mathematically distinct, permanently generated from their wallet address.

The art auto-evolves when transactions occur on mainnet. When the market moves, the specimens react. This is finance as aesthetics.

HOW IT WORKS

Each holder's wallet address is hashed into a deterministic seed for the generative art engine. No two specimens are alike — the visual output is a cryptographic fingerprint of ownership.

The system polls Solana mainnet every 15 seconds. When a new transaction is detected, the affected holder's art specimen automatically evolves — a flash, a redraw, a new form.

Seven distinct art algorithms are assigned by rank: Plasma Fields · Sacred Geometry · Orbital Systems · Flow Fields · Crystal Lattice · Mandala · Voronoi Cells.
